Instructions
For my creative assignment, I have chosen to create an interactive way for my peers to test their innate immune system knowledge.

While playing this game, it is highly recommended that your web browser is maximized and sound is turned on.

For my three pieces of work, there will be three stages, each consisting of four levels.

The objective of the game is to destroy the invading pathogen by answering a series of questions. To move on to the next stage and beat the virus, you must pass all four levels at each stage.


Good Luck and Enjoy!
