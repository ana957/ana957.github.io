Instructions
For our student outreach project, Team 10 has chosen to create an interactive way for undergraduate students with basic/sufficient knowledge in biology and psychology to learn about myths and misconceptions surrounding schizophrenia.

While playing this game, it is highly recommended that your web browser is maximized.

You will watch a series of six short animated videos, each tackling different myths and misconceptions that pelage the schizophrenia community.


Team 10: Kayanna Earle, Anish Shrestha, Cameron Goddard, Behina Malek Khosravi and Derick Tidort
Enjoy!
